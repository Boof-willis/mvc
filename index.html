[ Frequently Asked ]

The questions buyers ask first.

A quick look at pricing, zoning, and the build-out path. The full list lives on the FAQs page.

Mountain View Commons (MVC) is a 43-unit mixed-use flex space development in Heber City, Utah. Each unit is roughly 5,000 square feet across three floors with a 30' x 60' footprint per floor, drive-through garage doors on the ground level, and zoning for residential, commercial, and warehouse use under one roof.

Interior gray-shell units start at $1,050,000 (~$200/SF). End and duplex shells are $1,250,000 (~$250/SF). Finished, turnkey packages with a certificate of occupancy run $1,950,000 to $2,420,000 (~$400/SF) depending on the floor plan. Each phase releases at a higher price than the previous one.
