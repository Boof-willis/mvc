[ PLAN-01 / OUTLAW / MODEL UNIT OPEN ]

The
Outlaw.

Three bedrooms. Two baths. A full residential floor between the kitchen and the bedrooms. The drive-through garage stays. The Outlaw is the only finished model unit currently open at Mountain View Commons — it's the easiest way to see what 5,000 square feet of mixed-use actually feels like.

3 BEDS 2 BATHS ~5,000 SF FROM $2.42M
Walk the Model See the Layout →

[ Floor by Floor ]

Three levels. Three uses.

Garage on the ground floor. Kitchen, dining, and living on the middle floor. Three bedrooms and the primary suite up top. The Outlaw is the most residential of the four floor plans.

[ Level Detail ]

What lives where.

01

[ LV-01 / GARAGE ]

Drive in. Drive out.

20-foot ceilings, 16-foot roll-up doors at front and rear. Pull a long trailer in one side and out the other without unhitching. Bath on this level. Plenty of clearance for a lifted truck, a Sprinter conversion, a wakeboat on a trailer, or four snowmobiles parked side-by-side.

02

[ LV-02 / GREAT ROOM ]

Kitchen. Living. Dining.

The middle floor in The Outlaw is fully residential. Open kitchen with island, dining area, living room. The model unit's build-out features walnut cabinetry, brass hardware, a stone fireplace, and a half-bath off the entry. This is the floor you actually live on.

03

[ LV-03 / BEDROOMS ]

Three beds. Two baths.

The top floor holds the primary suite plus two additional bedrooms and a second full bath. Primary bath in the model has a marble shower, soaking tub, and double vanity. Big enough for a family. Quiet enough — three floors above the garage door — that you forget you're sleeping above one.

[ Who Buys The Outlaw ]

Most often, families.

The Outlaw is the residential heavyweight of the four plans. With three bedrooms upstairs and a full great room on the middle floor, it lives like a house — except the ground floor is a 1,800-square-foot garage instead of a basement.

01

Primary residence

Family with toys

Two parents, two or three kids. Skis in the winter, side-by-sides in the summer. The garage swallows the trailer, the Sprinter, and the bikes. The kids each get a bedroom upstairs.

02

Second home / mountain base

Wasatch Back getaway

A primary home elsewhere. The Outlaw is the long-weekend base camp 15 minutes from Deer Valley, with the boat parked downstairs. Nightly rental–permitted when you're not using it.
