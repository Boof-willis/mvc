Home/Floor Plans/The Frontier
[ PLAN-02 / FRONTIER / 2 BEDROOM ]

The
Frontier.

Two bedrooms, two baths, and a kitchen up top. A flexible professional floor in the middle. Drive-through garage with a bath underneath. The Frontier is the plan for owners who want real living space upstairs, but still want a working second floor — office, studio, or showroom.

2 BEDS 2 BATHS ~5,000 SF FROM $2.10M
Schedule a Tour See the Layout →

[ Floor by Floor ]

Garage. Office. Home.

The Frontier splits the difference between The Outlaw (mostly residential) and The Mercantile (all commercial). Park downstairs, work in the middle, live up top.

[ Level Detail ]

What lives where.

01

[ LV-01 / GARAGE ]

Toys, trucks, trailers.

Same drive-through garage as every other plan. 20-foot ceilings, 16-foot roll-up doors front and rear. Half-bath on this level so workshop hours don't require a trip upstairs. Room enough for a wakeboat, a side-by-side, and a Sprinter — at the same time.

02

[ LV-02 / PRO SPACE ]

Office. Studio. Showroom.

The middle floor in The Frontier is configured as professional space with a bathroom. Use it as a primary office, a creative studio, a private gym, or build it out for a small team. The flexibility is the whole point — keep it residential, make it commercial, lease it out.

03

[ LV-03 / RESIDENCE ]

Two beds. Two baths.

Full residential floor up top: two bedrooms with two bathrooms, kitchen with island, dining area, and living room. Big enough for a couple plus guests. Compact enough to feel like a refined mountain pied-à-terre rather than a sprawling house.

[ Who Buys The Frontier ]

Adventure owners with guests.

The Frontier is the most-balanced plan: enough garage to swallow the toys, enough work space for a real second floor, and enough residential up top for two people plus visitors.

01

Adventure / second-home

Couple with a fleet

Two bedrooms is enough — two adults plus weekend friends. The middle floor is the gear room, the bike workshop, or the home office. Garage holds the snow toys in winter, the boat in summer.

02

Remote professional + family

Working from the mountains

A founder, consultant, or operator who works full-time from a real office, not a kitchen counter. The Frontier puts the office on its own floor and the bedrooms on theirs. Calls upstairs don't wake the kids.
