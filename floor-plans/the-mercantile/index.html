[ PLAN-04 / MERCANTILE / ALL COMMERCIAL ]

The
Mercantile.

All commercial. No bedrooms. The Mercantile dedicates every floor to working space — drive-through garage at grade, open professional floor in the middle, and an executive level up top with a glass office. Built for trades, professional services, and small companies that need a real headquarters.

0 BEDS 3 BATHS ~5,000 SF FROM $2.05M
Schedule a Tour See the Layout →

[ Floor by Floor ]

Garage. Open floor. Executive level.

The Mercantile is the most-commercial of the four plans. Every floor is built around the workday — and the geometry of trades, sales meetings, and small-team operations.

[ Level Detail ]

What lives where.

01

[ LV-01 / GARAGE ]

Trucks. Equipment. Trades.

The garage is the entrance for the working day: drive-through 16-foot doors, 20-foot ceilings, room for a service truck plus equipment trailers. Bath on this level. Trades use this floor as fleet staging. Showrooms use it as oversized lobby and delivery bay.

02

[ LV-02 / OPEN FLOOR ]

One floor. Many seats.

An open professional floor with a bath, no interior walls in the standard build. Configure as a bullpen for a 6–10 person team, an open showroom, a fitness studio, or a workshop with finishing area. The open plan is the entire pitch — bring your own walls.

03

[ LV-03 / EXECUTIVE ]

Glass office. Conference. Bath.

The top floor is the executive level: open professional space, a private glass-walled office, and a third bathroom. Run the company from here, hold client meetings, or lease the level separately. With its own elevator stop, it can be sub-divided as a separate suite.

[ Who Buys The Mercantile ]

Trades. Professional services. Small companies.

The Mercantile is the only plan with no residential floor. Buyers are running a business under this roof.

01

Trades / contractors

Service-business HQ

Plumber, electrician, landscaper, mechanical contractor, AV installer. Trucks and trailers stage on the ground floor. Crews dispatch from the second. Owner runs the business from the glass office on three. Total cost beats most leased commercial space in Park City over a 7-year hold.

02

Professional services

Studio · agency · firm

Architect, designer, financial advisor, attorney, agency. Open floor for the team, executive level for partners and conference space. The garage doubles as covered parking for clients and storage for archives, samples, or photography gear.

03

Showroom + back of house

Retail / showroom

Furniture, automotive, sports gear, art. Ground floor as drive-in delivery and oversized lobby; second floor as primary showroom; third floor as offices and overflow display. With nightly rental–permitted residential floors elsewhere in the development, owners often pair a Mercantile with an adjacent Outlaw.

04

Investor / leaseback

Income property

Buy the Mercantile, lease it to a tenant business at market commercial rates. With Wasatch County as a 2034 Olympics host market and Heber's commercial supply nearly nonexistent at this price point, the cap rate math holds up at current basis.
